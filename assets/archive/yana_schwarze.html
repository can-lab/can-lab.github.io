Yana Schwarze

Trainee

I am very interested in understanding the mechanisms of stress and its effects on mental health, with the hope that this research this will be clinically relevant in the future.

For my internship I’m working on the DynaMORE project with Sophie Bögemann. DynaMORE is a multidisciplinary project, which aims to improve mental health and well-being and to provide resilience in the face of adversity. This is done by collecting psychological, behavioural, neural, and physiological markers in at risk-individuals during stressful life phases.

I obtained my bachelor’s degree in Cognitive Science in Osnabrück, Germany and I am now in the second year of my master in Cognitive Neuroscience in Nijmegen. Next to my studies I work as a research assistant for the healthy brain study where I am involved in the acquisition of MRI data.

In my free time I like to try out new foods and practice different sports like squash or bouldering.
