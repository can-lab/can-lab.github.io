Jelle Asbreuk

Trainee

Some of my research interests are mental health, stress and neurological disorders.

I am currently an intern working on the DynaMORE project with Judith van Leeuwen and Sophie Bögemann. DynaMORE aims to promote stress resilience and improve mental health and well-being in the face of adversity. This is done by collecting psychological, behavioural, neural, and physiological markers in at risk-individuals during stressful life phases.

I obtained my bachelor’s degree in Medical Biology here in Nijmegen. In 2020 I started with the master Medical Biology and I am currently doing the Neurobiology specialization.

In my free time I like to play squash, go to festivals, watch Netflix series and drink beers with friends.
