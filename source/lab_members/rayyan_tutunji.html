Rayyan Tutunji

PhD student

I am a PhD candidate here at the CANLAB, where I am part of an ERC funded project examining the dynamics of stress. More specifically, I will be looking into a theory proposed by Hermans et al (2014, TINS) that predicts shifts in three large networks in the brain as a function of stress. I will further look into between subject differences that might be predictive of daily life stress reactivity based on continuous physiological monitoring and ecological momentary assessment in day to day life.

My previous work had a wide range of focus, with earlier work in stress, perception, and childhood development.
