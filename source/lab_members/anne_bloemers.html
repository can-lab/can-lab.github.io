Trainee

I am an intern in the Cognitive Affective Neuroscience lab. I will be working with Rayyan Tutunji on the Burnout project and in this study I will focus my research on the influence of the Allostatic Load on Burnout. My research interests are in the field of stress-related psychopathology. What are the mechanisms behind the onset of stress and how can this be translated to the clinical practice.

I have completed my Bacherlor’s program in Medicine at the Radboud University. This internship is part of my Medicine Master’s program.
