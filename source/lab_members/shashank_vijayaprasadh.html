My main research interests lie around Motivation, Cognitive control and stress on one side, I would also want to learn and work more with neurological (movement) disorders such as Alzheimer's, Epilepsy, and Parkinson’s disease.

As a first step towards this, I have joined as an intern in the DANDY project where we try to dissociate the roles of Dopamine and Noradrenaline during a stress response using ‘several tasks’ (Can’t say what they are Ssssshh) which can be later useful in creating methods of interventions for PTSD, Depression and many other stress related disorders.
